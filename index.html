About Our Company

Hermitage Multi Venture Ltd. is a dynamic, Nigeria-based company committed to transforming lives and communities through diverse business operations. As the parent company of Hermitage Bakery, Hermitage Oil & Gas, and Hermitage Football Club, and the proud sponsor of the Nat and Jovi Foundation, we are dedicated to creating sustainable solutions that uplift struggling Nigerians by providing employment, empowerment, and essential services.
